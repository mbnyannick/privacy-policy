Privacy Policy for Video Text Extractor

Last updated: [Current Date]

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. We do not track users or gather any information about your browsing habits.

Local Processing Only

All video text extraction occurs locally within your browser. Video frames are processed temporarily for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and immediately discarded. No video content or extracted text is sent to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension only stores your preference settings (such as toggle on/off state) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your computer.

Third Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.
