Copytext Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Simple Promise: Copytext respects your privacy completely. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data.

What We Don't Collect

Copytext does not collect, store, transmit, or track:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Video content
  • Extracted text
  • Usage analytics
  • Any user data whatsoever

How It Works

All video text extraction happens locally in your browser. Video frames are processed temporarily for OCR and immediately discarded. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Local Storage Only

The only data stored is your copy mode preference (on/off toggle) which stays on your device using Chrome's local storage API.

Third Parties

Copytext uses no third-party services, analytics, tracking, or data collection tools of any kind.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
